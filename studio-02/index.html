Backpack

My backpack is my toolbox. It is a small collection of items that encapsualte who I am. My backpack allows me to move from place to place and still be able to function. I chose a Timbuk2 backpack because all my friends have one, and because it is a great company that is very conscience about sustainability and green energy. A great thing about having a backpack is that it is not a static object. I personalize my backpack with pins (music pins mostly i.e. one of the Smiths - my favorite band - and one of KDVS - the Davis radio station) and keychains (I have a David Bowie - Ziggy Stardust and the Spiders From Mars one currently on the zipper).

Notebook

My notebook is another piece of my mind. In a sense, it's my old fashioned external hard drive for my brain. I write down all my ideas, my notes, my drawings, my to-do list, my everything. I know moleskines are the hottest notebooks to use, super trendy, but I like Leuchtturm 1917 notebooks best. Nothing against moleskines though, they're great too, I have a few of those as well.

iPhone

My iPhone. Literally goes everywhere with me, thus, it is included in my everday carry. It is hard to picture how I would go about my day without it. It is how I communicate and connect. It's my phone, my camera, my bank, and so many other things. It is one of the most powerful tools I carry. Understandably, it is considered an addictive technology that is destroying this generation, but it is so much more than that.
